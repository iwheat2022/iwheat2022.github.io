Outdoor Activities



In order to strengthen students' physical fitness and interest in team sports activities, under the teacher's careful planning, the laboratory's sports activities are carried out in an orderly manner every Friday. Mainly focus on a variety of sports activities; to stimulate students' interest in sports, create a strong atmosphere of laboratory unity, and cultivate students' collaboration ability, health awareness, and collectivism spirit. Every Friday afternoon sports event is decided by a common vote on Thursday. Sports for basketball, volleyball, football, badminton, swimming and so on.
