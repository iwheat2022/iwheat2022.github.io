Yanli Feng, breeder of dryland wheat variety -- 'Tongmai' series, was invited to have a discussion on how to choose employment for postgraduates.


日期
2022-02-25 12:00 PM — 5:30 PM

Expert Profile:
Yanli Feng, female, senior agronomist, is currently the deputy head of the Yaozhou District People’s Government of Tongchuan City and the deputy chairman of the Tongchuan Municipal Committee of the Democratic National Construction Association. Since 1991, he has been engaged in wheat breeding and technology promotion in the front line of agricultural production. He participated in the breeding of ‘Tongmai No. 1-3’ series of dryland wheat varieties, and presided over the breeding of ‘Tongmai No. 4-6’ series of dryland wheat varieties. He has presided over and participated in 24 provincial and municipal scientific research and technology promotion projects, of which four won the Municipal Science and Technology Progress Award issued by the municipal government, and five won the Provincial Science and Technology Promotion and Progress Award issued by the provincial government, and were rated as outstanding in Tongchuan City. Contributed top-notch talents and winner of the “May 4th” Youth Medal in Shaanxi Province.
forum:
On February 25, 2022, Yanli Feng, a breeder of the dryland wheat variety ‘Tongmai’ series, came to the research group to have a discussion and exchange on how graduate students should choose jobs and find employment. All graduate students in the laboratory participated in the discussion.
At the symposium, Feng Yanli carefully shared her study and work experience, and provided three valuable experiences to the students: First, always cherish the thirst for knowledge, maintain a progressive heart, be full of thirst for knowledge, and constantly motivate yourself 2. We must do well the seemingly complicated, subtle and ordinary daily work, exercise our confidence and patience with priorities, work hard for a long time in the process of thinking, exploration, and summarization, improve day by day, and strive for early accumulation. Think, improve emotional intelligence, if you want to win the respect of others, you must first know how to respect others. If you want to be warm and kind, you must first learn to treat people with sincerity.
Afterwards, the students had a more in-depth exchange with Feng Yanli about their career and employment confusion. There were heated discussions around ‘how to choose a work direction’, ‘how to choose a work place’ and ‘how to define a job as a career to pursue in a lifetime’. Ma Xiuyun, a 2019 graduate student, said happily: ‘The communication with Mr. Feng was like a timely spring rain, which blew away the ‘smog’ I faced in choosing a career. Now I am more clear about what career choice I should make.’ Sun Shuyang, a graduate student, said: ‘Thanks to Mr. Feng for his valuable advice on employment issues, which allowed me to determine the goals of my postgraduate studies and provide direction for my future study and work.
